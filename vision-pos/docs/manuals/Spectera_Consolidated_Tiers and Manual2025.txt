Provider Manual Highlights (June 2025 Edition)

Section 4.9 – Formularies
- Progressive lenses: 5 categories (Tier I–V)
- Anti-reflective coatings: 4 categories (Tier I–IV)
- Non-formulary products default to “All Other Ophthalmic Lens Options” reimbursement level.
- Digital/free-form single-vision and blended bifocal lenses are not included.
- Formularies are found at spectera.com → Formulary Lists.

Section 4.2 – Full Service Plans
- Includes exam, frame allowance, lenses, and lens add-ons.
- Standard lens: CR-39 plastic or glass.
- Polycarbonate, high-index, and enhancements add patient cost.
- Contacts may be selection (formulary) or non-selection (allowance).

Section 4.10 – Provider Warranty
- One-time no-charge prescription change within 90 days.
- One-time no-charge replacement for defective lenses or frames within 1 year or manufacturer warranty.

Section 5.4 – Member Responsibility
- Copays and lens options calculated automatically during online order/claim.

Section 8 – Claims
- Submit within 90 days via spectera.com or EDI (Payer ID: 00773).
- Non-formulary AR/progressives billed under “All Other Ophthalmic Lens Options.”

Section 9 – Standards of Care
- Follow AOA guidelines; dilation required for diabetic members; CPT II encouraged for HEDIS.
